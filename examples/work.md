# Work Index

> Where your past writing lives, plus a one-line index of each piece. This is the agent's map for two things: calibrating your **voice** (it reads a few in full before drafting) and checking **consistency** (it reads the ones adjacent to a new topic). It's also where new pieces get logged after each session.
>
> Copy this file to `profile/work.md`. Point `sources` at real writing; the more there is, the sharper the voice and the fewer questions the interview needs.
